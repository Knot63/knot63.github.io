The Process

  • Opportunity Analysis
  • Prototyping
  • Integration
  • Strategy

Based on your product needs, we identify opportunities for Machine Intelligence within your existing data. We help your product team understand the potential and limitations of Machine Intelligence.

After identifying opportunities, we use lean prototyping to determine what’s possible and achievable.

We answer questions of whether the amount and type of data is sufficient, what the lower and upper performance bounds for an in-house solution will be, what results will look like and what the user experience can be.

We build and test several solutions to present the best options available and help product teams understand the potential and limitations of machine intelligence.

We make Machine Intelligence part of your company's core competence. You own the important product decisions while we assist your engineering team in building production systems in- house. We ensure that your Machine Intelligence solutions are understandable and maintainable for the future.

Machine Intelligence teams thrive on strong structure, process, and skill set definition. We teach industry-leading process such as A/B testing, model version management, product science, data acquisition techniques, feature estimation, and hiring practices.

We also offer team advisory retainers for further strategy.
